# E-commerce Sales Analysis & RFM Segmentation

# Project Overview

This project performs an end-to-end analysis of a real-world Brazilian E-commerce dataset. The goal was to extract actionable business insights from transactional data and identify high-value customer segments using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.

# Business Objectives

- Analyze total revenue and sales trends
- Evaluate delivery performance
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Perform customer segmentation using RFM
- Apply Pareto (80/20) analysis to understand revenue concentration

# Key Insights

- Total Revenue: 15.4M+
- Total Delivered Orders: 96,478
- Average Order Value: 159.83
- Average Delivery Time:12 days
- Top 10 product catego ries contribute significantly to overall revenue
- 49% of total revenue comes from a small percentage of customers (Pareto principle)

# Customer Segmentation (RFM)

Customers were segmented into:

- Champions  
- Loyal Customers  
- Potential Loyalists  
- At Risk  
- Others  

This segmentation enables targeted marketing strategies and retention planning.
