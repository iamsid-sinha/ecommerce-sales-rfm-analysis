# E-commerce Sales Analysis & RFM Segmentation

##  Project Overview

This project performs an end-to-end analysis of a Brazilian E-commerce dataset (100K+ transactions) to extract actionable business insights from transactional data and segment customers using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.

The objective was to understand revenue trends, delivery performance, product performance, and customer value distribution.

##  Business Objectives

- Analyze total revenue and monthly sales trends
- Evaluate delivery performance and delays
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Perform RFM-based customer segmentation
- Apply Pareto (80/20) analysis to understand revenue concentration

##  Key Insights

- Total Revenue: 15.4M+ BRL
- Total Delivered Orders: 96,478
- Average Order Value (AOV): 159.83 BRL
- Average Delivery Time: ~12 days
- Nearly 49% of revenue is generated by a small percentage of customers (Pareto effect)
- Health & Beauty, Watches & Gifts, and Bed, Bath & Table are top revenue-generating categories

##  Customer Segmentation (RFM)

- Champions
- Loyal Customers
- Potential Loyalists
- At Risk
- Others

This segmentation enables targeted marketing strategies and retention planning.

##  Analysis Performed

- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Delivery performance and delay calculation

- RFM score computation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- Pareto (80/20) revenue concentration analysis
