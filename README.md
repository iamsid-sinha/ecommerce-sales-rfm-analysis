# 📊 E-commerce Sales Analysis & RFM Segmentation

## 📌 Project Overview
Performed an end-to-end analysis of a Brazilian E-commerce dataset (100K+ transactions) to extract actionable business insights and segment customers using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.

Analyzed revenue trends, delivery performance, product performance, and customer value distribution to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze total revenue and monthly sales trends  
- Evaluate delivery performance and delays  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Perform RFM-based customer segmentation  
- Apply Pareto (80/20) analysis to understand revenue concentration  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Total Revenue: **15.4M+ BRL**  
- Total Delivered Orders: **96,478**  
- Average Order Value (AOV): **159.83 BRL**  
- Average Delivery Time: **~12 days**  
- ~**49% of total revenue** generated by a small segment of customers (Pareto effect)  
- Top categories: **Health & Beauty, Watches & Gifts, Bed, Bath & Table**  

---

## 👥 Customer Segmentation (RFM)
Customers were segmented into:
- Champions  
- Loyal Customers  
- Potential Loyalists  
- At Risk  
- Others  

➡️ Enabled targeted marketing strategies and customer retention planning
